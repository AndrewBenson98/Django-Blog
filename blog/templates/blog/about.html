About

Thank you for checking out my blog application!

This Django App was made by following a YouTube tutorial by Corey Schafer.

This is a simple blog application that lets users register, login/logout and write blog posts.

Users can also update their profile pictures and email adresses. Images are stored using 
the AWS S3 service.

If users forget their password, there is a reset password feature that will send you an 
email to reset your password.

By clicking on a user, you can see all the blog posts that user has written.
Posts can also be deleted and edited but only by the author of the post.
